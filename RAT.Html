Rats are various medium-sized, long-tailed rodents.
Rats are typically distinguished from mice by their size.
Rat meat is a food that, while taboo in some cultures, is a dietary staple in others.
Some rats even have jobs such as sniffing out gunpowder residue, acting and even even animal assisted therapy.
Rats have a very good sense of smell and are easy to train.

The the most common places to find rats

  1. Trash can
  2. Under a bridge
  3. At school playgrounds
  4. Sewers
  5. labs
