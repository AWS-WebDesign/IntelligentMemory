INFO ON RATS

These powerful small creatures are ferocious in every aspect.

More specific types of rats: Brown rat, Black rat, Long-haired rats, Bush rat, Ricefield rats, Polynesian rats, Bulldog rat, Palm rat, Malayan field rat, Austrilian swamp rat, Opossum rat, Tanezumi rat, there are many more but these are just a few. Rats are various medium-sized, long-tailed rodents. Rats are typically distinguished from mice by their size. Rat meat is a food that, while taboo in some cultures, is a dietary staple in others. Some rats even have jobs such as sniffing out gunpowder residue, acting and even even animal assisted therapy. Rats have a very good sense of smell and are easy to train
